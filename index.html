Ace's Breed.

Welcome to the website about Ace! First we will talk about Ace's breed. Ace is actually a mutt mixed with multiple breeds.

HOUND

A hound is a type of hunting dog used by hunters to track or chase prey.

LABRADOR

The Labrador is a breed of retriever dog from the United Kingdom that was developed from imported Canadian fishing dogs.

PITBULL

The American Pit Bull Terrier is a medium-sized, intelligent, short-haired dog, of a solid build, whose early ancestors came from the British Isles.

Ace's Food

The picture is Ace's food and water bowl. That is where he eats breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Ace has now become a picky eater. When he was just a puppy, he would eat kibble all the time. Now his tastes have changed.

Ace will refuse to eat only kibble. He must have some kind of meat or canned food mixed with the kibble. My family often makes chicken or steak to mix with his kibble. Ace is a spoiled boy!
